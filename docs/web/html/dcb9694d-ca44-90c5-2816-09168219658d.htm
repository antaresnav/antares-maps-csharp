CameraPositionCameraPositionBuilderZoom Method

Sets the zoom level of the camera. Zoom level is defined such that at zoom level 0, the whole world is approximately 256dp wide.Increasing the zoom level by 1 doubles the width of the world on the screen.Hence at zoom level N, the width of the world is approximately 256 * 2 N dp, i.e., at zoom level 2, the whole world is approximately 1024dp wide. When changing the camera position for a map, the zoom level of the camera is restricted to a certain range depending on various factors including location, map type and map size. Use AntaresMap.getMinZoomLevel and AntaresMap.getMaxZoomLevel to find the restrictions. Note that the camera zoom need not be an integer value.
